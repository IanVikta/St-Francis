How to Apply

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form, providing your personal details, academic history, and program of interest. Ensure all required fields are filled accurately to avoid delays.

2

Send Documents

Submit official transcripts, recommendation letters, and any additional materials specified for your chosen program (e.g., test scores, essays, or portfolios). Documents can be sent electronically or mailed to:

3

Interview Process

Some programs may require an interview to assess your fit and goals. You'll be contacted to schedule a virtual or in-person interview with our admissions team. Prepare to discuss your aspirations and how St. Francis aligns with them.

4

Receive Decision

Once your application is reviewed, you'll receive a decision via email and mail. Accepted students will also receive details on next steps, including enrollment, financial aid, and orientation.
